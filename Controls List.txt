Controls List

Interactions

To use any button on the menus simply click on the required icon

Camera

Camera movement is done using the mouse or trackpad
Zoom in and out using the scroll wheel
Change camera angle by pressing Z

Putting

Putting is done by holding down left click.
Release left click to putt the ball.
To cancel putting, continue to hold left click until the power bar resets.

Pausing

To pause the game press the "Escape" key
